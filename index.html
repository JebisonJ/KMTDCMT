About Us

Kumbalathu Mahadevar Temple Devotees Chellaiyan Memorial Trust is a spiritual and community-focused charity dedicated to preserving traditions, supporting the underprivileged, and fostering unity. Rooted in the values of service and devotion, we strive to make a positive impact in Kanyakumari District and beyond.

What We Do

Social Welfare

We organize food drives, health camps, and educational support for the needy in our community.

Spiritual Activities
