About Our Trust

Kumbalathu Mahadevar Temple Devotees Chellaiyan Memorial Trust (KMTDCMT) is a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving tradition, fostering spiritual growth, and uplifting our community. Rooted in the values of compassion and service, we strive to make a positive impact through various charitable initiatives and cultural activities.

  • Supporting education and healthcare for the underprivileged
  • Organizing cultural and spiritual events
  • Promoting unity and harmony in the community
  • Preserving the rich heritage of our temple and traditions
Contact Us

Our Mission

To unite devotees and well-wishers in meaningful service, nurture spiritual values, and empower the less fortunate, ensuring a brighter and more harmonious future for all.
